Events in the year 2025 in the Czech Republic.

Incumbents

[edit]
  • President – Petr Pavel
  • Prime Minister – Petr Fiala

Events

[edit]
  • 11 January – Seven people are killed in an explosion and subsequent fire at a restaurant in Most.[1][2]
  • 20 February – Two people are killed in a knife attack at a shopping center in Hradec Králové. A 16-year old suspect is arrested.[3]
  • 28 February – A freight train carrying benzene derails and catches fire in Hustopeče nad Bečvou. No injuries are reported.[4]
  • 21 March – representatives of SPD, Svobodní, Tricolour and PRO sign a memorandum about cooperation in the 2025 parliamentary election with SPD as an election leaders.[5]

Scheduled

[edit]
  • 18–29 June – EuroBasket Women 2025 in the Czech Republic, Germany, Greece and Italy[6][7]
  • October – Next Czech parliamentary election
  • 5–14 December – 2025 Women's World Floorball Championships[8][9]

Deaths

[edit]

January

[edit]
  • 5 January – František Šmahel, 90, historian.[11]
  • 10 January – Milan Feranec, 60, politician, MP (since 2017).[12]
  • 11 January – Mario Klemens, 88, conductor.[13]
  • 16 January – František Makeš, 93, Czech-born Swedish artist.[14]
  • 18 January – Eliška Wagnerová, 76, judge and politician.[15]
  • 21 January – Petr Hannig, 79, musician and politician.[16]

February

[edit]
  • 4 February – Jiří Čtvrtečka, 82, Olympic canoeist.[17]
  • 16 February – Vladimír Válek, 89, conductor.[18]
  • 17 February – Petr Matoušek, 75, cyclist.[19]
