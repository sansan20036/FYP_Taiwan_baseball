Exhibitions

[edit]
  • January 10 until March 16 - Nick Cave: Amalgams and Graphts at the Jack Shainman Gallery in New York City.[2]
  • January 18 until June 1 - Gertrude Abercrombie: The Whole World is a Mystery at the Carnegie Museum of Art in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.[3]
  • February 8 until May 11 - Caspar David Friedrich: The Soul of Nature at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City.[4]
  • February 14 until June 15 - Anselm Kiefer: The Early Works at the Ashmolean Museum at Oxford University in Oxford, England.[5]
  • February 18 until March 8 - Sylvie Covey: Botanical Space at the nohp M55 Gallery in New York City.[6]
  • March 6 until April 19 - Walton Ford: Tutto at Gagosian in New York City.[7]
  • April 9 until August 10 - Amy Sherald: American Sublime at the Whitney Museum of American Art in New York City.[8]
  • April 9 until August 31 - David Hockney; 25 at the Louis Vuitton Foundation in Paris, France.[9]

Deaths

[edit]

January

[edit]
  • January 3 – La Chunga, 87, Spanish flamenco dancer, painter. and painter's muse (Salvador Dali) (born 1938)[10]
  • January 4
    • Ed Askew, 84, American painter (born 1940)[11]
    • Peter Brandes, 80, Danish painter and sculptor (born 1944)[12]
    • Elisabeth Haarr, 78, Norwegian textile artist (born 1945)[13]
  • January 4 – Jagdish Mittal, 99, Indian art collector (born 1925)[14]
  • January 5 – Philippa Blair, 79, New Zealand artist (born 1945).[15]
  • January 7 – Leo Segedin, 97, American painter (born 1927)[16]
  • January 8 – Alastair MacKinven, 53, British artist (born 1971)[17] (death announced on this date)
  • January 13 – Oliviero Toscani, 82, Italian photographer (Benetton) (born 1942)[18]
  • January 16
    • David Lynch, 78, American film director and visual artist (born 1946)[19]
    • George A. Tice, 86, American photographer (born 1938)[20]
  • January 17 – Jules Feiffer, 95, American cartoonist, author, and visual artist (born 1929)[21]
  • January 21
    • Jo Baer, 95, American painter (born 1929)[22]
    • Håkon Bleken, 96, Norwegian painter (born 1929)[23]
    • Renina Katz, 99, Brazilian engraver (born 1925}[24]
  • January 22 – Aaron De Groft, 59, American art museum director (Orlando Museum of Art) (born 1965).[25] (death announced on this date)
  • January 24
    • Rutherford Chang, 45, American conceptual artist (born 1979)[26]
    • Jaune Quick-to-See Smith, 85, American artist and curator (born 1940)[27]
  • January 27 – Alonzo Davis, 82, American artist and academic (born 1942)[28]
  • January 28 – Graham Nickson, 78, British painter, longtime dean of the New York Studio School; (born 1946)

February

[edit]
  • February 1 – C. Richard Kramlich, 89, American venture capitalist and video art collector (born 1935)[29]
  • February 2 – Helga de Alvear, 88, German-Spanish art collector and dealer (born 1936)[30]
  • February 3
    • David Edward Byrd, 83, American graphic artist (born 1941)[31]
    • Lim Tze Peng, 103, Singaporean painter (born 1921)[32]
  • February 5
    • Satoru Abe, 98, American sculptor and painter (born 1926)[33]
    • Waldo Díaz-Balart, 93, Cuban painter and sculptor (born 1931)[34]
  • February 8 – Yrjö Kukkapuro, 91, Finnish interior architect and furniture designer (born 1933)[35]
  • February 9 - Walter Robinson, 74, American painter and art writer (born 1950).[36]
  • February 10 - Mustafa Arruf, 66, Spanish sculptor (born 1958)[37]
  • February 11 - Kay Smith, 101, American visual artist (born 1923)[38]
  • February 12 - Mel Bochner, 84, American conceptual artist (born 1940)[39]
  • February 20 - Frankétienne, 88, Haitian painter (born 1936)[40]
  • February 23 - Ming Fay, 82, Chinese-born American sculptor (born 1943)[41]

March

[edit]
  • March 1 - Jack Vettriano, 73, Scottish painter (The Singing Butler) (born 1951)[42]
  • March 3 - Victor Cicansky, 90, Canadian sculptor (born 1935)[43]
  • March 14 - Fred Eversley, 83, American sculptor (born 1941)[44]
  • March 17 - Peter Sedgley, 95, English artist, co-founder of Space Studios (born 1930)[45]
  • March 22
    • Joe Goode, 87, American painter.[46]
    • Asaf Lifshitz, 82, Israeli sculptor (born 1942)[47]
  • March 27 - Marcia Marcus, 97, American painter (born 1928)<ref_https://www.nytimes.com/2025/04/06/arts/marcia-marcus-dead.html</ref>
