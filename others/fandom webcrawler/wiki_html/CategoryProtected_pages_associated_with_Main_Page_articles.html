This category contains images and templates that are protected because they are or will soon be in articles linked from the Main Page. Unlike templates and images directly on the Main Page, templates and images in articles linked from the Main Page are only sometimes protected, as deemed necessary.

Instructions for administrators

[edit]

The {{mprotected2}} template should be applied to images and templates on the English Wikipedia.
