About me

[edit]

I am a Wikipedian that joined mid-2011. I currently possess the administrator and global renamer user rights. As such I can perform administrative tasks such as editing pages with any level of protection, blocking and unblocking users, deleting, or undeleting pages as well as protecting them. I also can rename any user account globally on all Wikimedia wikis. If you need me to carry out an administrative task, just shoot me a message and I'll be happy to help. In addition to the administrative tasks, I can of course perform tasks of other users that aren't administrators too.

I'm easy to get along with, and find myself reasonable, if you feel I made a mistake, please let me know. I'm always willing to listen to reason. I tend to AGF a lot, giving people another chance, even though many feel it's undeserved, but as a result if that chance is wasted, there will be no redemption afterwards. I do my best to get along with users I encounter on Wikipedia. Being able to work on Wikipedia doesn't only require community trust, but an ability to get along with users.

I primarily focus on the technical aspects of Wikipedia. I specialize in bot work, developing tools that benefit the community and hopefully makes their lives easier here. With my elevated permissions I also work at WP:ACC assisting new users with getting started on Wikipedia. I also review requests to rename on Meta, the rename queue, and WP:CHUS.

If you need my assistance in my area, I am always willing to help. I have adept knowledge of many policies on Wikipedia, and general understanding of the remaining policies. I am always willing to help, so just leave me a message on Wikipedia.
