Events in the year 2025 in Liechtenstein.

Incumbents

[edit]
  • Prince: Hans-Adam II
  • Regent: Alois
  • Prime Minister: Daniel Risch

Events

[edit]
  • 1 January – Same-sex marriages comes into effect in Liechtenstein.[1]
  • 9 February – 2025 Liechtenstein general election: The Patriotic Union retains its plurality in the Landtag.[2]
  • 3 April – Radio Liechtenstein ceases broadcasting following an October 2024 referendum that saw voters approve ending state funding to the station.[3]
