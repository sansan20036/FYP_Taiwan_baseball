Events in the year 2025 in Andorra.

Incumbents

[edit]
  • Co-Princes: Emmanuel Macron and Joan Enric Vives Sicília
  • Prime Minister: Xavier Espot Zamora

Holidays

[edit]
Further information: Public holidays in Andorra

Source:[1]

  • 1 January - New Year's Day
  • 6 January - Epiphany
  • 3 March – Carnival
  • 14 March - Constitution Day
  • 18 April - Good Friday
  • 21 April - Easter Monday
  • 1 May - International Workers' Day
  • 9 June - Whit Monday
  • 15 August - Assumption Day
  • 8 September - National Day
  • 1 November - All Saints' Day
  • 8 December - Immaculate Conception
  • 25 December - Christmas Day
  • 26 December – Saint Stephen's Day
