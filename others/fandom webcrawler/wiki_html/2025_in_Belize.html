The following lists events in the year 2025 in Belize.

Incumbents

[edit]
  • Monarch: Charles III
  • Governor-General: Froyla Tzalam
  • Prime Minister: Johnny Briceño
  • Chief Justice: Louise Blenman

Events

[edit]
  • 22 February – Three US nationals are found dead in a suspected drug overdose at the Royal Kahal Beach Resort in San Pedro Town.[1]
  • 12 March – 2025 Belizean general election: The ruling People's United Party wins 26 seats in the House of Representatives, with the opposition United Democratic Party winning the remaining five seats in the chamber.[2]

  • 13 October – Indigenous Peoples’ Resistance Day

  • 26 December – Boxing Day
