List of 10,000 most vital articles on the English Wikipedia

Overview
Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4 Level 5
Shortcuts
  • WP:VITAL4
  • WP:VA4

The five nested vital article Levels are meant to give direction to the prioritization of improvements of English Wikipedia articles (e.g. which articles to bring to WP:GA and WP:FA status), to provide a measurement of quality of overall English Wikipedia (e.g. what proportion of the most important articles are at GA and FA status), and to serve as a centralized watchlist of English Wikipedia's most important articles. Unlike the list of articles every Wikipedia should have, they are tailored to the English Wikipedia and are actively maintained by the dedicated WikiProject Vital Articles. This page contains links to the 10,000 articles of the Level 4 list.

Any modification to these lists should ONLY BE MADE after a discussion on the Level 4 talk page.
