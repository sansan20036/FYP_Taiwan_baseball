Hello, I'm Dilettante (née Novo Tape). I spend most of my time monitoring recent changes via AntiVandal (with occasional forays into copyediting), so most of my edits are reverts and user warnings. If I reverted your edit and you don't know why, please leave a message on my Talk page (or a thread on your talk page, if it exists) and use your judgement to decide whether or not you should undo my edit. If you feel I've failed to properly explain an edit, please leave a message.

If you look to the right, you'll notice a variety of userboxes. Some of them directly pertain to editing enwiki and others are just hobbies.

Disclosures

[edit]

I have never edited Wikipedia or any other Wikimedia projects for pay. Besides the accounts linked on this page, I have not, to the best of my knowledge, edited under any other accounts. I have used IPs while not logged in in the past, but such edits were all in good faith and I cannot locate most of the IPs. I'd estimate such edits number less than two dozen.
