This is a timeline documenting events of jazz in the year 2025.

Events

[edit]
  • January 29 – February 1 – The Bodø Jazz Open takes place in Norway, featuring Solveig Slettahjell, Arve Henriksen. Rebekka Bakken and others.[1]

Albums

[edit]
Month Day Artist Album Label Notes Ref.
June 14 Daniel Carter Stream of a Dream 577 Records Featuring Matthew Putman and Federico Ughi [2]

Deaths

[edit]
  • January 12 – Mark Izu, American double bass player and composer, 70[3]
  • January 14 – Teddy Osei, Ghanaian saxophone player, drummer and vocalist, leader of Osibisa, 87[4]
  • March 4 – Roy Ayers, American jazz-funk vibraphonist, record producer, and composer, 84[5]
  • March 8 – Bill Ashton, British saxophonist, band leader and composer, 88[6]
  • March 14 – Bruno Romani, Italian saxophonist, flutist and composer, 65[7]
  • April 1 – George Freeman, American guitarist, 97[8]
