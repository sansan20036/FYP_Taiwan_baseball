Administrators! Yes, you!

Please read the selected anniversaries guidelines before making your edit, and write each listing in the past tense.

  • This page usually defers to supporting articles for accuracy or when there is disagreement, so it is best to achieve consensus and make any necessary changes there first. After updating, please purge the cache of the Main Page so that the updated version appears.
  • If you are adding a new event, please also list it in the Events section of the corresponding April 8 article. All new events that are added here should also comply with the criteria for inclusion listed on Wikipedia:Days of the year.
  • Events listed on the Main Page are chosen based more on relative article quality and to maintain a mix of topics, not based solely on how much their subjects are important or significant. Generally, only five to six events are posted at a time, and thus not everything that is "most important and significant" can be displayed at the same time. Thank you.
