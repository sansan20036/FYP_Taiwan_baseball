Events

[edit]
  • 21 February – Tajikistan and Kyrgyzstan announce an agreement to delineate their common border.[1]
  • 2 March – 2025 Tajik parliamentary election (Assembly of Representatives): The ruling People's Democratic Party of Tajikistan wins 49 of 63 seats in the chamber.[2]
  • 28 March – 2025 Tajik parliamentary election (National Assembly)[3]
  • 31 March – Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan and Uzbekistan sign an agreement fixing their common border's tripoint.[4]
